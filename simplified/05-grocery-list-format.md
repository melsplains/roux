# Grocery List Output Format

This file is a reference — it shows you what the grocery list will look like when Claude generates it, and explains how to use it.

You don't need to attach this to your Claude prompt. It's here so you know what to expect before you get started.

---

## What Claude's grocery list looks like

```
## Produce
- [ ] bananas — 1 bunch
- [ ] broccoli — 1 large head (or 2 crowns)
- [ ] romaine lettuce — 1 bag
- [ ] cherry tomatoes — 1 pint

## Meat & Protein
- [ ] salmon fillets — 4 pieces (about 1.5 lbs)

## Dairy & Eggs
- [ ] shredded Mexican cheese — 1 bag
- [ ] sour cream — 1 container
- [ ] butter — 1 lb (getting low)
- [ ] chocolate milk — 1/2 gallon

## Pantry & Dry Goods
- [ ] pasta sauce — 2 jars
- [ ] chicken broth — 32 oz carton
- [ ] taco seasoning packet

## Bread, Wraps & Crackers
- [ ] sub/hoagie rolls — 1 pack of 6
- [ ] wheat bread — 1 loaf

## Frozen
- [ ] frozen pizza x1 (backup stock)
- [ ] frozen broccoli — large bag

## Snacks & Drinks
- [ ] applesauce pouches — 1 pack
- [ ] granola bars
- [ ] Diet Pepsi — 2 liters

## Household
- [ ] paper towels — 1 pack
- [ ] trash bags — kitchen size

---
Estimated total: ~$145–$165
Shared ingredients this week: diced tomatoes (chili + taco night), shredded cheese (tacos + Friday pizza)
```

---

## How to use the list

**Copy it to your phone:** After Claude generates the list, you can copy the whole thing into a notes app (Apple Notes, Google Keep, etc.) and check things off as you shop.

**Copy into AnyList:** Open AnyList on your phone, go to your grocery list, and paste each item in. AnyList will auto-sort by category.

**Use it as-is in Claude:** The list stays in your Claude chat. Just keep the window open while you shop and check items off mentally.

**Print it:** Copy the list, paste into a word processor, print. Old school but works.

---

## How to tell Claude to update your inventory after you shop

Once you're back from the store, you can ask Claude to update your inventory file based on what you bought. Just paste your inventory file into a chat and say:

> "Here's my current inventory. I bought everything on this list: [paste your grocery list]. Please update the inventory to reflect what I now have."

Claude will give you back an updated inventory you can copy-paste over your old file.

---

## Notes on format

The grocery list Claude produces won't have headers formatted exactly like the template above every time — it adapts based on what you actually need to buy. If some sections are empty (like "Meat & Protein" when the freezer is fully stocked), Claude will skip them.

If the format isn't working for you, just tell Claude:

> "Format the grocery list as a plain list with no sections — just one item per line with a checkbox."

Or:

> "Give me the grocery list sorted by aisle order at Kroger / Walmart / [your store]."

Claude will adjust.
