## Camping Rules

- **First night = simplest meal.** Arrival day is chaos — hot dogs, burgers, or something prepped at home. No exceptions.
- **Prep at home before leaving.** Chop vegetables, assemble foil packets, pre-mix anything mixable. Camp is for cooking, not prepping.
- **Freeze proteins before the trip** — they double as ice packs and thaw over the trip. Plan frozen-protein meals for the later days.
- **No meals that need precise timing or lots of dishes.** One pot, one packet, or one grill grate.
- **Return day:** plan an easy dinner at home (frozen backup or leftovers) — nobody cooks a real meal after unpacking.

## Always on the Camping Grocery List

- S'mores: graham crackers, marshmallows, chocolate bars
- Hot dogs + buns, travel-size condiments
- Heavy-duty foil, paper plates, paper towels
- Ice (note it on the list — buy day-of)
